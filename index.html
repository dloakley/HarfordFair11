Directions

From the South (Scranton)

     Take Route 81 North to Harford Exit 217, at end of ramp turn left. Go approximately 1 mile to stop sign. Turn right up hill. Fair grounds at the top of the hill.

From the North (Binghamton)

     Take Route 81 South to Harford Exit 217, at end of ramp turn right. Go approximately 1 mile to stop sign. Turn right up hill. Fair grounds at the top of the hill.

GPS: 485 Fair Hill Rd, New Milford, Pa. 18834

On some GPS simply enter Harford Fair Grounds, Harford, Pa
